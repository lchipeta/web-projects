Enthusiastic, determined, well organised mum of two who has a keen interest in art and crafts. I have grown to love children's art since my son joined nursery. It's amazing what little ones can produce on a sheet of paper within a short space of time and no advance planning. I think their imagination and creativity is beautiful, spontaneous and out of this world. In my opinion, it deserves a bit more appreciation than just ending up on the fridge or their bedroom walls only to be discarded and forgotten when they hit the teenage years hence the birth of 'Tods color splash'. A great way to share, educate, inspire and archive children's early years work of art.
