About me

I am a mum of two with a keen interest in art and crafts. I love children's art. I think their imagination and creativity is amazingly and surprisingly beautiful, spontaneous and out of this world.

Children's art deserve a bit more appreciation than just ending up on the fridge or their bedroom walls only to be discarded when they reach teenage years hence the birth of 'Tods color splash'. A great way to share, educate, inspire and archive children's early years work of art.
