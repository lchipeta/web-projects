What is Tods color splash?

It's a fun, vibrant and colorful place for toddler's artwork. Whether it is scribbled with a pencil, pen, crayons or indeed kids paint, we welcome it all.

Here at Tods color splash, your online toddlers art gallery and exhibition center, we value childrens creativity and imagination. Parents, carers and guardians can share their little ones handiwork. It is not for sale but for free. We aim to showcase your childrens masterpieces to the world for fun, inspiration and education.

How does it work?

Fetch your toddlers drawings or paintings, take photos, upload them on our site and we will make sure to share them publicly for you. Images will be added to our online gallery and archived with your consent for future reference, in case you lose your hard copies. We recommend that you add your child's name ONLY on the painting for identification purposes. Please do not send family and friends pictures and remember it is free of charge so we will not ask for your payment details. At Tods color splash we endevour to maintain data protection for all our members in compliance with the UK GDPR guidelines. Please refer ICO.
